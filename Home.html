About CityClean

Working together for a cleaner, greener city

Who We Are

CityClean is a public service initiative focused on effective waste collection, recycling, and eco-friendly waste management solutions. We partner with local authorities, businesses, and citizens to create a sustainable city environment.

Our Mission

To build a cleaner and healthier future by reducing waste, promoting recycling, and ensuring responsible disposal practices for households and businesses alike.
